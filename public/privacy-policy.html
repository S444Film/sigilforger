Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sigil Forge ("the Website", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website at sigilforger.com.

Summary: Sigil Forge does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your sigils are generated entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to any server.

Information We Do Not Collect

Sigil Forge is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • The text you enter to create sigils
  • The sigil images you generate

All sigil generation happens locally in your browser. Your intentions and desires remain completely private.

Purchases

Sigil Forge offers optional purchases to unlock features:

  • Single Sigil Unlock (£0.99) — One-time purchase to download a single sigil
  • Lifetime Access (£9.99) — One-time purchase for unlimited sigil downloads

All purchases are processed by Stripe. We do not have access to your payment information or credit card details. For information on how Stripe handles your payment data, please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Sigil Forge stores the following data locally in your browser only:

  • Purchase status — Whether you have purchased Lifetime Access (stored in your browser's localStorage)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Clearing your browser data will remove this information.

Third-Party Services

Sigil Forge uses the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — For secure payment processing
  • Vercel — For website hosting

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Sigil Forge does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we do not collect information from children.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any user data, there is no risk of your information being compromised through our systems. All data remains in your browser under your control.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You have full control over any sigils you create — they exist only in your browser until you choose to save or share them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
