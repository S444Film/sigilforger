Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sigil Forge ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Sigil Forge does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your sigils are generated entirely on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

Information We Do Not Collect

Sigil Forge is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • The text you enter to create sigils
  • The sigil images you generate

All sigil generation happens locally on your device. Your intentions and desires remain completely private.

In-App Purchases

Sigil Forge offers optional in-app purchases to unlock features:

  • Single Sigil Unlock — One-time purchase to download a single sigil
  • Lifetime Access — One-time purchase for unlimited sigil downloads
  • Monthly Subscription — Recurring subscription for unlimited sigil downloads

All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. For information on how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Subscription Terms

If you choose the monthly subscription:

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscription automatically renews monthly unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID Account Settings
  • Any unused portion of a free trial period (if offered) will be forfeited when you purchase a subscription

Data Storage

Sigil Forge stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Purchase status — Whether you have made a purchase (stored securely via Apple's StoreKit)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Sigil Forge does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party service involved is:

  • Apple App Store — For app distribution and in-app purchase processing

Children's Privacy

Sigil Forge does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we do not collect information from children.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any user data, there is no risk of your information being compromised through our systems. All data remains on your device under your control.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You have full control over any sigils you create — they exist only on your device until you choose to save or share them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
