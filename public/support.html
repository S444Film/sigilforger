Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a sigil?

Enter your intention or desire in the text field (for example, "I am confident" or "I attract abundance"). Click "Begin the Ritual" and watch as your words are transformed into a unique geometric symbol. The process follows the traditional chaos magick method: vowels are removed, duplicate letters eliminated, and the remaining consonants are mapped to sacred geometry.

Why does my sigil have a watermark?

The preview version includes a watermark. To download your sigil in full HD resolution (2000×2000 pixels) without the watermark, click "Unlock & Download" and choose a purchase option: Single Sigil (£0.99) or Lifetime Access (£9.99).

What's the difference between the purchase options?

Single Sigil (£0.99) — Unlocks one sigil for download. Perfect if you just need one specific sigil.

Lifetime Access (£9.99) — Unlimited sigil downloads forever. Create as many sigils as you want, whenever you want. Best value if you plan to create multiple sigils.

Will the same intention always create the same sigil?

Yes. Sigil Forge uses a deterministic algorithm — entering the exact same text will always produce the exact same sigil. This is by design: your sigil is YOUR unique symbol for that intention.

What if I lose my Lifetime Access?

Lifetime Access is stored in your browser. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, you can contact us at support@super444.com with your Stripe receipt email and we'll help you restore access.

How do I request a refund?

For refund requests, contact us at support@super444.com. Please include your receipt or the email you used for purchase. Note that refunds for Single Sigil purchases are not available once the sigil has been downloaded.

How do I use my sigil?

Traditional practice involves three steps: First, meditate on the sigil until you can visualise it clearly. Second, "charge" it during a moment of focused concentration or emotional intensity. Third — and most importantly — forget about it. The sigil works best when your conscious mind releases attachment to the outcome.

You can print your sigil, set it as a wallpaper, carry it with you, or incorporate it into any personal practice that resonates with you.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your intentions and sigils are generated entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. We don't track what you type, and we have no access to your sigils. Payments are processed by Stripe — we never see your card details. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
