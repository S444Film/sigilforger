SUPPORT

How do I create a sigil?

Open the app and type your desire or intention in the text field. Tap "Begin the Ritual" to watch as your words are transformed through the traditional process: vowels are removed, duplicate letters are eliminated, and the remaining consonants are mapped to geometric forms to create your unique sigil.

Why is there a watermark on my sigil?

The preview version includes a watermark. To download your sigil in full HD resolution without the watermark, tap "Unlock & Download" and choose one of the purchase options: single sigil unlock, monthly subscription, or lifetime access.

What's the difference between the purchase options?

Single Sigil (£0.99) — Unlocks the current sigil only. Perfect if you just need one.

Monthly (£2.99/month) — Unlimited sigil downloads while subscribed. Cancel anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Lifetime (£9.99) — One-time payment for unlimited sigils forever. Best value if you plan to create multiple sigils.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device's Settings → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → Sigil Forge → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

If you purchased Lifetime Access or have an active subscription, open the purchase sheet and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Why does the same text create the same sigil?

This is intentional. Each desire produces a unique, deterministic sigil — your personal symbol. The same intention will always generate the same glyph, making it truly "yours".

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Is my data private?

Yes. Sigil Forge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your desires and sigils remain entirely on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
